About Our Club

Welcome to the Origami Club! We're a community of students passionate about the ancient art of paper folding. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced folder, our club offers a welcoming space to learn, create, and share beautiful origami designs.

From simple cranes to complex modular structures, we explore various folding techniques and challenge ourselves with new designs each week. Join us to develop patience, precision, and creativity while making new friends who share your interest in this timeless art form.
